### Fundamentals of Science Communication
###### A Short Course
_Siddharth Kankaria_

<details class="callout callout-note" open>
<summary class="callout-title">About the Course</summary>
<div class="callout-body">

Science communication is an act of connecting people with knowledge, emotions and shared experiences within the field of science. It is both an art to be practised creatively as well as a science to be studied systematically.

This week-long course will provide an introduction to the theory and practice of science communication and outline some fundamental tenets of communicating science strategically. Using interactive approaches like discussions, games, and activities, this course will provide Early Career Researchers with an introduction to the frameworks, formats and importance of science communication, and equip them to design, implement and evaluate their science communication efforts effectively.

</div>
</details>

### Teaching Plan

**Day 1**

- Defining SciComm & Public Engagement
- SciComm Strategy & Fundamentals
- Exercise: Choose a SC & PE project and develop a strategy zine for it

**Day 2**

- History of Science Communication
- SciComm Models
- Exercise: Write a 200 word piece describing a suitable model for your SC & PE project

**Day 3**

- Importance of Narratives for SciComm + Storytelling techniques for SciComm
- Dealing with Jargon + Metaphors & Analogies + Balancing Accuracy vs. Accessibility
- Exercise: Storyboarding a concept for your SC & PE project

**Day 4**

- Ethics of doing SciComm
- Understanding your SciComm Audiences
- Exercise: Write a 300-word profile of a specific target audience for your SC & PE activity

**Day 5**

- Exercise: Present your SC & PE project overview with reflections from course modules
- Challenges of doing SciComm in India
- Building a SciComm Career in India
