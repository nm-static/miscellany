### Teaching Plan

<img src="/images/docs/kasuti-embroidery/kasuti-pattern-1.jpg" alt="kasuti-pattern-1.jpg" style="width: 400px; max-width: 100%;" />

<div class="callout callout-tip">
<div class="callout-title">Session 1: Beginning with the Grid</div>
<div class="callout-body">

Introduction to Kasuti, understanding counted thread/grid logic, materials and setup, and first simple patterns.

</div>
</div>

<div class="callout callout-tip">
<div class="callout-title">Session 2: Learning the Stitches</div>
<div class="callout-body">

Foundational Kasuti stitches, rhythm, counting, and symmetry. Practicing repetition.

</div>
</div>

<div class="callout callout-tip">
<div class="callout-title">Session 3: Reading Patterns</div>
<div class="callout-body">

How to "read" a Kasuti pattern, translating from graph to fabric, and working with simple motifs.

</div>
</div>

<div class="callout callout-tip">
<div class="callout-title">Session 4: From Pattern to Form</div>
<div class="callout-body">

Combining motifs, scale and spacing, and beginning a small composition.

</div>
</div>

<div class="callout callout-tip">
<div class="callout-title">Session 5: Translation and Interpretation</div>
<div class="callout-body">

Moving beyond traditional motifs, translating simple images/forms into grid-based patterns, and thinking of embroidery as "drawing with thread."

</div>
</div>

<div class="callout callout-tip">
<div class="callout-title">Session 6: Your Own Piece</div>
<div class="callout-body">

Developing a small final work, slowing down, refining, sharing and reflection.

</div>
</div>
