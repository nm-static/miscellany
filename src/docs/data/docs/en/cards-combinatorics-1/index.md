## Cards and Combinatorics

**Year:** 2019
**Code:** SC-235
**Instructor:** Manish Jain (and team)

### Overview

A fun and engaging course that explores combinatorial mathematics through the lens of card tricks and magic. Students learn mathematical concepts while developing skills in card manipulation.

### Topics Covered

1. Introduction to Card Magic
2. Permutations and Combinations
3. The Gilbreath Principle
4. Perfect Shuffles and Group Theory
5. Information Theory in Magic
6. Self-Working Card Tricks

### What You'll Learn

- Mathematical principles behind card tricks
- Basic card handling techniques
- How to design your own mathematical card tricks
- Connections between magic and computer science

### Prerequisites

- Curiosity about mathematics
- A standard deck of playing cards
- Willingness to practice!
